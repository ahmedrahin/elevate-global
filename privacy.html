Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Privacy Policy

At Elevate Global Group, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website, products, or services.

  • Personal Information

    This includes your name, contact information, and any other data you provide when contacting us or using our services.

  • Usage Data

    We collect information about your interaction with our website and services, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

  • Cookies

    We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and gather analytics to improve our services. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

You have the right to

  • Review, correct, or update your personal information.
  • Request the removal of your data from our systems.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
How We Use Your Information
  • 1 To provide and maintain our services.
  • 2 To improve our services and enhance your experience
  • 3 To communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries and providing updates.
  • 4 To personalize and tailor our offerings to your preferences.
  • 5 To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

  • Data Security

    We employ stringent security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. Our commitment to data security includes the use of encryption, regular security audits, and access controls.

  • Disclosure of Your Information

    We may disclose your information to third parties only when necessary for the provision of our services or to comply with legal requirements. We do not sell or trade your information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

  • Third-Party Links

    Our website may contain links to third-party websites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these websites before providing any personal information.
